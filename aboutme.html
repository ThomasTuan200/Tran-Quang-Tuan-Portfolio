My Stories & Journeys

I am introverted and stoic person. I have learned a lesson that sometime you need to take actions to gain more experience and to enrich my perspective. During my life journey, I find myself that I love Graphic Design and I want to design a product that could help other convey their message delegately.

I am learning at multimedia school known as ARENA MULTIMEDIA. I have learned and sharpened many basic to advanced skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, and Logo Designing on first semester. However, during my semester two, I had discovered another passion in Web Designing. Specifically I am more impressed in how collected insights are convey into real product that bring true value. Therefore, I am learning how to do Web Design in semester two and my upcoming goal is to become a proficient UX/UI Designer.


My specialties are designing Point Of Sales Materials, Stationary Design, Logo Design, Branding Guideline, Website Coding and UX/UI projects.
